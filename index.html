Understanding the impact of technology interventions on carers

We are doing a study that aims to gain a better understanding of how technology can be used by carers during their caring sessions. We aim to use these findings to develop better technology that can respond and manage the needs of the carer in a more efficient and effective way. We would like to invite you to participate in this research project.
